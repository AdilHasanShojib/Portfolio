002 : SwiftEats

GitHub Link

SwiftEats is an intuitive and efficient food delivery platform.

Tools & technologies used:Tools & technologies used:Next.js, Node.js, MongoDB, API, HTML, CSS

  • It is an intuitive and efficient food delivery platform where restaurants, users, and delivery personnel come together.SwiftEats offers a seamless experience for food enthusiasts, restaurant owners, and delivery personnel.
  • Restaurant Owners: Add, edit, and delete food items to manage their menu efficiently.
  • Users: Search for restaurants by name or city, order food, track order history, and view total bills.
  • Delivery Personnel: Accept orders based on city and check their delivery history.
